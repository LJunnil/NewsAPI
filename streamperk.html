✦ Travellers Also Ask

How many days in the Philippines is enough?

A short 4-day stay covers a city or two, but at least 2 weeks is recommended — most bloggers suggest 3 weeks to see Palawan, Boracay, and Bohol properly.

What is the best month to go?

December to February is the sweet spot — warm 75–88°F temperatures, sunny skies, and minimal rain. Shoulder months (Nov, Apr) offer fewer crowds.

✦ Safety & Planning

Is the Philippines safe for tourists?

Major tourist areas like Palawan, Boracay, and Cebu are generally safe. Exercise caution in Mindanao and the Sulu Archipelago. Always check your government's latest advisory.

Top blogs to follow for planning?

Salt in our Hair, Bucketlist Bums, Stoked to Travel, The Travelista, and Wandering Earl all have detailed 2026 Philippines guides.
